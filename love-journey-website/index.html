Our Story

Our paths first crossed on a sunlit summer day in May of 2007. She was riding her bicycle, and when she paused to converse, I felt an immediate and profound connection with her. That moment was nothing short of magical. Yet, despite the special bond I sensed, we knew nothing of each other's lives or whereabouts. It took twelve long years for fate to bring us together again. On a rainy day in September 2019, we reconnected through Facebook Messenger. Our conversation flowed effortlessly and lasted for four enchanting hours. It was an experience that felt both surreal and extraordinary. From that moment, I knew deep within my heart that I had fallen in love with her. This, indeed, is how our beautiful love story began.
